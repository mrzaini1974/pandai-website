Alhamdulillah, the official website of Pertubuhan Amal & Da'wah Anak Istimewa (PANDAI) is now live.

Through this site you can follow our latest news and activities, browse the programme gallery, check the activity calendar, and find information about the MAIWP Autism Centre, now operated by PANDAI.

This website is built with accessibility features to be friendly for all visitors, including those with visual or attention-related needs. Try the blue button at the bottom corner of your screen to adjust the display to your comfort.
